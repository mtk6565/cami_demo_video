# Cami AI — Video Ad Voiceover Script

**Duration:** 47 seconds | **Format:** ElevenLabs v3 TTS
**Audience:** Receptionists at pet businesses (WhatsApp distribution)
**Tone:** Friendly, relatable, empathetic — like a coworker who gets it
**Pacing:** ~3.5–4 words/sec | **Total word count:** ~170 words

---

## Notation

- `...` — natural breath pause (~0.3s)
- `[pause]` — longer pause (~0.7s)
- **bold** — words to emphasize (slightly slower, warmer)
- `// note` — delivery direction (not spoken)

---

## SCENE 1: HOOK (0:00–0:04)

// Conversational, warm. Like you're sitting next to them at the front desk.

> You know the drill... Clients messaging on WhatsApp, calling, DM-ing on Instagram — **all at once**. [pause] What if you never had to juggle that again?

---

## SCENE 2: WHATSAPP FLOW (0:04–0:43)

### Phase 1 — Booking Request (0:04–0:08)

// Matter-of-fact, easy.

> A client messages your business on WhatsApp... **Cami** picks it up instantly.

### Phase 2 — Cami Reply (0:08–0:13)

// Slightly impressed, like showing off a cool trick.

> She greets them by name, checks your calendar, and offers open slots — all in **seconds**.

### Phase 3 — Slot Pick (0:13–0:18)

// Relief. Contrast with the chaos.

> They pick a time. No back and forth... no phone tag. [pause]

### Phase 4 — Deposit (0:18–0:23)

// Punchy, confident. Each phrase lands.

> Deposit? Collected right there in the chat. Slot locked. **Done.**

### Phase 5 — Confirmation (0:23–0:28)

// Knowing smile energy. Hits the pain point.

> Twenty-four hours before the appointment... Cami sends a reminder **automatically**. No more no-shows.

### Phase 6 — Grooming Pics (0:28–0:33)

// Lighter, warmer. A nice moment.

> During the visit, share photos with the client — they **love** seeing their pet getting pampered.

### Phase 7 — Thank You (0:33–0:38)

// Satisfied, wrapping up.

> After the groom, Cami follows up — asks for a rating, invites a review. All **hands-free**.

### Phase 8 — Repeat Invite (0:38–0:43)

// Building to the close. This is the "wow" moment.

> And a month later? Cami brings them back with a personal offer. Your rebooking... on **autopilot**.

---

## SCENE 3: OUTRO (0:43–0:47)

// Measured, confident. Let each word land.

> **Cami.** [pause] Your entire pet business — **automated**, on **WhatsApp**.
